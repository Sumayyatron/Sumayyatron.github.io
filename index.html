I’m a San Diego-born UCSD graduate, writer, and former nonprofit professional aiming to transform my 10+ years of CRM database management, website development, and analytics-informed communications into a data analytics career

About Me

My analytic mind informs my work in the arts and humanities. I'm the kind of wonky nerd who makes worldbuilding spreadsheets of all her 400+ fictional characters, watches and reads essays on city planning for entertainment, and thinks that the symbolic logic class required for my undergraduate Philosophy degree was "pretty fun, in retrospect!" I'm a bit of a jack-of-all-trades in that I have worked in everything from event security to trail maintenance to clothing retail to scene painting to grantwriting to election judging, but I am determined for my next step to take me into the world of data analytics. I hope to remain there for years while continuing to write and publish speculative fiction that envision the better world that I aim to help create. I'm passionate about diversity in tech, data ethics, New Urbanist Memes, and would love to work for any SD-local or remote organization that uses data to better the world.
